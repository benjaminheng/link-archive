When the AON Evade was first launched as the Recon 9, a 21L backpack, it quickly became a popular crowdsourced choice. Over time, it evolved into the 2.0 (30L) and the recent 1.5 (25L), but now, it has come full circle back to its original 21L size in the form of our first Evade 1.0X.

Product development is a collaborative effort, and the Evade 1.0X represents a significant step forward thanks to the valuable feedback from existing Evade owners and the dedicated contributions of remarkable individuals and collaborators…………..our dear friends and esteemed industry pros, David Valencic and Brandon Vulaj.

But what does the "X" signify?

The "X" commemorates the brand's 10th anniversary, coinciding with the 20th anniversary of our parent company, SERT. During its initial decade, AON was dedicated to creating products based on crowdsourced ideas and feedback. Our foundation in law enforcement and military gear production remained an integral part of this process. However, what's lesser-known is my personal background in the fashion industry, where I spent nearly 20 years contributing to prestigious brands like Armani, Kenzo, Comme des Garcons, Dolce & Gabbana, Karl Lagerfeld and DKNY (my first product development gig).

As we celebrate AON's 10th anniversary, we're proud to unveil the X series, starting with the Evade 1.0X. This time, together with assistance from Brandon & David, we're infusing my personal fashion sensibilities into the designs, merging what I think is the best of both worlds.

Warm regards.......HELM

The Evades 1.0X will be made available in batches on a first come, first serve basis, starting with the X50 variant. And yes there will only be one type, no more Fulls nor Lites.

Current DATA:

  • Volume: 21L
  • Weight: 3.5 lbs (1.575 kg)
  • Dimensions: 18.5” (H) x 12” (Base) x 6” (D) (47 x 30.5 x 14 cm)

Shell: Dimension-Polyant’s X50, bottom of pack 1050D ballistic nylon

Interior: Fully lined in Challenge’s UltraGrid Dark Chili

Reinforced carry handle with with leather wrap

Zipper:

  • YKK TPU Coated Reverse Coil #10 and #8; *bottom front zipper is #8 RC
  • Leather zipper pulls

Webbing: Multi-column (aka car seat)

Main compartment:

  • Front wall has our 2 zippered pockets design (top fabric, bottom mesh)
  • Back wall holds the padded & suspended laptop and tablet sleeves.
  • Back wall has a single layered PALS row at the top to allow use of Matroskya and other pouches.

Laptop Compartment:

  • While the laptop sleeve is part of the main compartment, it is sealed off thus creating it's own compartment with access only via the top quick access zipper that's behind the carry handle.
  • This seal is hook & loop and can adjusted +/- 0.5" to lower a slightly small or larger opening (front to back).
  • Tested to fit up to a current 16" MacBook Pro.

Front pockets:

  • Front organizer compartment has 2 sleeve pockets with large loop field, and a zippered pocket above it.
  • Loop field is sized to hold existing Type 1 & 2 organizers and any hook & loop compatible pouch.
  • Alternatively you can slip one of our Sliders in there.
  • Top front quick access pocket
  • Bottom stealth pocket with lip covering reverse coil zipper

Back panel and shoulder / strap harness is an updated version of the Whitley with new SBR close cell foam in the shoulder straps.

Flat side water bottle pockets; currently this has only been tested to hold the Hydroflask Trail (24oz).

Let's address some common questions:

  • Q: Is the 1.0X a limited release?
  • A: As with our previous launches, the 1.0X will be limited initially. Based on demand, feedback, and resonance with our audience, it could become a staple in our offerings.
  • Q: Will the 1.0X be available in other fabrics?
  • A: For now, it will only be offered in the X-Pac and UltraGrid combination. We're working on streamlining our product range, including fabrics and color choices.
  • Q: Will the 1.0X come with alternative lining colors?
  • A: No, the current lineup features a singular lining color.
  • Q: Where will the 1.0X be available?
  • A: The following stockists are expected to carry the launch:

Rushfaster - rushfaster.com.au (Australia / NZ)

Surburban - suburban.com.hk (Hong Kong)

Vesta - in-store purchase only (HCMC, Vietnam)

Additional notes: 

There is no CCW compartment on this Evade.
There are no compression straps.
There are no loop patches on this pack apart from the removable leather patch.

All our products are developed and manufactured in our own Studio in Saigon, Vietnam using materials sourced from the US, Korea, Taiwan and domestically.
