The level 1 of the Gateway@klia2 mall is dedicated as the Transportation Hub for the klia2 airport. At this level, you can find various travel options – bus services, taxi services, car rental services and the boarding platforms for Express Rail Link (ERL) train services – for you to utilize and move on to your next destination.

The Transporation Hub is located at Level 1 of Gateway@klia2 mall

If you prefer to stay near the klia2 airport to catch your next flight, here is a list of hotels near KLIA and klia2 for your review.

The Transportation hub is located at level 1 of the Gateway@klia2 mall. Once you exit from the Arrival Hall which is connected to level 2 of the Gateway@klia2 mall, just walk straight and follow the overhead signboards.

Travel options at Transportation Hub
  • Bus Services – skybus, aerobus, star shuttle, etc
  • Car Rental Services – avis, hertz, europcar, etc
  • Driving to klia2 – highway to use, road map, etc
  • ERL Train Services – klia ekspres and klia transit
  • Taxi Services – metered & coupon based taxi
  • Where is klia2 Airport – location and address

Also you can find short-term lodging services for you to take a rest if you are in transition.

Express Rail Link (ERL) Trains

The train ticket for ERL (KLIA Ekspres and KLIA Transit) train services is available for purchase at ERL station located on Level 2 of the Gateway@klia2 mall.

Travellers will buy the ticket and then go down to Level 1 to board the ERL train.

Ticket counter at ERL station on Level 2 of Gateway@klia2 mall

At the klia2 ERL station, you have these ERL options:

  • KLIA Transit – 39 minutes from klia2 to KL Sentral
  • KLIA Ekspres – 33 minutes from klia2 to KL Sentral
  • KLIA to klia2 via KLIA Transit or KLIA Ekspres – 3 minutes

With the KLIA Transit, the total distance for the entire rail track from klia2 to KL Sentral is 59km and the journey time is 39 minutes.

KLIA Transit route map

Once you are at the boarding platforms for the ERL train, observe that platform A is for KLIA Ekspres and platform B is for KLIA Transit.

Driving your vehicle to klia2

If you are driving to klia2 airport, any of the following highways can be used:

  • North-South Expressway (PLUS)
  • Guthrie Corridor Expressway (GCE)
  • New Klang Valley Expressway (NKVE)
  • Shah Alam Expressway (KESAS)
  • Lebuhraya Damansara-Puchong (LDP)
  • South Klang Valley Expressway (SKVE)
  • Maju Expressway (MEX)

To connect to North-South Expressway Central Link (ELITE) then exit on KLIA Interchange (E608) and head to KLIA direction on KLIA Expressway.
