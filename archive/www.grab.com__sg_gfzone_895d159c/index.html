Deliver with GrabFood and enjoy flexible working hours – no shifts, no schedules and no delivery location limitations! You can, however, give your earnings an additional boost, by picking up orders from merchants within high demand zones. These zone and time based incentives vary weekly; check your email or view in your Grab app for full details.

Familiarise yourself with the GrabFood zones – be savvy and maximise your earnings! Delivery zones are applicable to all jobs across GrabFood, GrabMart and GrabExpress FnB.

GrabFood delivery zones was last updated on 11 Dec 2021. Please view the detailed map for clarity.

Note that these zone changes are for zone boost incentives only and are not applicable for shift zones.
Please refer to your shift details in Grab Driver App > Booking Planner for full information on each individual shift, including the shift areas.
