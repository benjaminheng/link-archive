It is a chromoly handle with an original stem of the new brand "FAIR WEATHER" named "Fair weather".

A product created through joint development with "NITTO", which produces many high-quality cycle parts.

Known as Macchan Bar.

The beautiful silhouette unique to the Blumous bar, in which the pipe divided into two from the column part connects to the handle, is wonderful.

It is designed for NITTO UI-2 stem with 90mm protrusion and a flat bar with a back sweep of 15 degrees.

The material is made of tough chromoly and the welding is carefully made by skilled NITTO craftsmen.

The handle width is as wide as 710mm, and combined with the back sweep, it is very comfortable to grip and has excellent operability.

It is the best match for FAIRWEATHER CX FRAME and SURLY bikes.

It is a handle that makes mountain play more fun.

Perfect for MTB, of course, cyclocross bikes and city riding everyday bikes.
