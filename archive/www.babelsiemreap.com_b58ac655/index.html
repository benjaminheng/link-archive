WELCOME

Operating for more than ten years, Babel Guesthouse is an Eco-friendly guesthouse with focus on responsible tourism. With 22 spacious rooms we offer our guests a peaceful atmosphere in our tropical garden with a charming restaurant, serving vegan-friendly food. We also have Cambodia´s very first Eco-shop and Refill Station, offering Eco-friendly alternatives to unnecessary plastics in your daily life.

We are doing our best to cause as little damage as possible to the area in which we operate and we are constantly seeking to reduce our impact on the environment.

22 spacious rooms with a green and tropical vibe

We are proud to present Cambodia´s first “Zero Waste” Bulk Shop in our lobby area.

Our vision is to make it easier for travelers who wish to reduce their traveling footprint to choose plastic-free alternatives. In our Eco-shop you´ll find various items perfect to bring on your travels to reduce the use of single use plastic while traveling. Most of these Eco-friendly items are locally made, creating jobs for locals.

We constantly seek to provide benefits from the tourism industry to the local community. Our local staff and Tuk Tuk drivers receive good working conditions, plenty of training, on site English lessons and the possibility to be sponsored with higher education.

The restaurant is surrounded by a tropical garden, serving a combination of local-, western-, and vegan-friendly dishes
