Pilot GrabFood Shifts System!

Introducing Grab’s new and improved In-App Shift Pilot System for Delivery-Partners on Motorbikes, Bicycle and PABs! We have taken on your feedback and you can now make shift bookings more easily within your Grab Driver app. Read on to find out more, sign up for your first shift and be prioritized!

⚠️ No action is required from Delivery-Partners who do not wish to sign up for shift pilot system. You can still go online and receive orders as per normal. 

Important Dates & Information

Duration: Now till 31 March 2022
Zones: Downtown+Bukit Merah, Lower Central+Upper Central, Southeast, Southwest, East, Jurong East

Please take note that changes in vehicle types for shift eligibility will take up to 3 working days to reflect.
For example: Alex, an Emerald Walker, is not eligible for any shifts. He decides to change to Cycle. He will only see the peak shifts in his booking planner 3 working days later.

How Does Shift Booking Work?

  1. Tap here or on your profile pic
  2. Tap on Bookings Planner to view the available shift timings and zones
  3. Select the shift/shifts that you want to join

Please Note: The cutoff time for booking a shift is 2 hours before its start.

  1. Tap “My Bookings”
  2. Tap on the shift you want to release
  3. Tap on release my slot

Please Note: You can only release your shift up to 2 hours before the start of the shift

Perks & Benefits of Shift Booking

  • ZONAL LOCK IN! You will only get orders where the pick up AND the DROP OFF is WITHIN the shift zone.
  • PRIORITY ALLOCATION You will get priority allocation for jobs during the shift timing, so you can hit your quests faster!
  • EARNINGS STABILITY: You will get a Minimum Trip Fare guarantee for the shift period. If you earn below the guarantee, we will top it up to the guaranteed amount in the following week.

Minimum Fare Guarantee (MFG)

What does a Fare guarantee mean?
This refers to the total Fare (amount that you earn on the app, exclusive of zone boost and quest incentives) that you earn for the entire designated shift period. Please refer to your booking planner for details on each shift’s minimum trip fare guarantee.

Example 
John (on Bike) earns a total of $37 for his 3 hour lunch shift in Central zone on a weekday. The minimum fare guarantee for that shift is $39. A top up of $2 will be processed to his cash wallet within 6 hours after completion of shift, provided he met all the requirements for the top-ups. However, if John earns $45 for the designated shift, he will not get any top up as he has earned more than the guaranteed amount. 

All other incentives remain the same and are in addition to the trip fares.

Requirements for Minimum Fare Guarantee/Bonus
In order to receive the Minimum Fare Guarantee top-up/Bonus, delivery-partners are to maintain the following: 

  1. Minimum of 90% acceptance rate & Maximum of 10% cancellation rate during the shift
  2. Online for a minimum of 90% of the entire shift duration. Please refer to the following important points on eligible online hours:
    • Auto-Accept must be toggled ON
    • MyDestination must be toggled OFF
    • Only hours that you are online within the shift zone will be counted
    • Both GrabFood & GrabMart service type must be toggled ON

More information on the various zones here. Please note that if you have opted-in for zonal fleet, you will need to opt-out of it before you can try out the shift booking system. 

No Shows, Strike & Appeal System

A no-show takes away the slot from another Delivery-Partner. If you are unable to make it for your shift, do remember to release your booking. 

Delivery-Partners who do not show up for their booked shift(s) or achieve less than 50% of their shift will receive strikes and reminder messages from Grab. A 3-strike rule has been implemented and delivery-partners who receive 3 strikes in a week will be banned from booking a shift for the following week. Strikes are refreshed on a weekly basis.

For example, John still did not show up for his shifts even after receiving reminder messages and received the message on Monday, 28th December, informing him that he is banned from booking shift(s) for the following week of 4th to 10th January. He can only book shift(s) again from 11th January onwards.

Delivery-partners found out to be attempting possible fraud will be suspended from GrabFood platform.

  2. Have successfully opted-out of zonal fleet (if you previously opted-in)
  3. Toggle:
    • OFF My Destination
    • ON Auto-Accept
    • ON both GrabFood & GrabMart service types
  4. Ensure you have >$0 in your cash/credit wallet
  5. Do not linger at 1 location for too long and move around to hotspot areas within the zone

You need to make sure that you toggle on all the shift service types for your online hours to be captured.

For example, if the shift has GrabFood and GrabMart services and you only toggle on GrabFood, your online hours will not be captured and you might be recorded as no-show for the shift. In this case, you need to have both GrabFood and GrabMart service types toggled ON for duration of shift.
