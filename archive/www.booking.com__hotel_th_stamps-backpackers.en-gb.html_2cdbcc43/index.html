Hostel Stamps Backpackers

1/11 Chaiyapoom Road, T.Chang Moi, Amphoe Muang Chiang Mai, Si Phum, 50300 Chiang Mai, Thailand – Excellent location - show map

You're eligible for a Genius discount at Stamps Backpackers! To save at this property, all you have to do is sign in.

Located in Chiang Mai, Stamps Backpackers offers dormitory rooms with a semi-private sleeping loft. Free WiFi is available on site. Guests can socialise and meet new people at the common area.

Dormitory rooms feature either air conditioning or a fan. Each bed is equipped with two power outlets for guests' conveniences. There are also a large backpack locker, a personal reading light and a private curtain. All guests have access to the shared bathrooms.

Wat Chiang Man is 750 yards from the property, while Tha Pae Gate is 700 yards away. The nearest airport is Chiang Mai International Airport, 3.1 miles from Stamps Backpackers.

This is our guests' favourite part of Chiang Mai, according to independent reviews.
