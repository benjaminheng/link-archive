The KLIA Transit ERL train is a fast train service with quick stop between klia2 ERL station, KLIA ERL station, Salak Tinggi ERL station, Putrajaya & Cyberjaya ERL station, Bandar Tasik Selatan ERL station, and KL Sentral ERL station. The total journey time from klia2 to KL Sentral is 39 minutes.

ERL train on the move

The KLIA Transit train accommodates frequent departure with frequency of every 15 minutes during peak hours, and every 30 minutes during off-peak hours.

Overview

  • Train schedule from klia2 to KL Sentral (Monday – Friday)
  • Train schedule from klia2 to KL Sentral (Saturday – Sunday)
  • Train schedule from KL Sentral to klia2

The first KLIA Transit train from klia2 ERL station starts at 5:18am and it takes 39 minutes to reach KL Sentral ERL station.

The ERL train can travel at a maximum speed of 160km per hour, and carry a full load of 540 passengers.
