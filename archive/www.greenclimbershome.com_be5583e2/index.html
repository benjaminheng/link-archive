Rock Climbing in Asia - We offer...

Climbing & Bouldering

You find wonderful routes with tufas, sintering and face climbing. The gigantic roof with routes ranging from 6b+ to around 8a+ is unique. So far there is a total of around 400 routes ranging from 4 to 8c in the valley. Whether advanced climbers or beginners, you will have a lot of challenge and amusement!

Accommodation

Located directly at the base of the climbing area “Pha Tam Kam”, the cozy bungalows cater to all climbers needs with hot water showers and comfortable beds. We have 20 bungalows and a camp ground in both Green Climbers Homes and additionally 12 beds in the dorm in Green Climbers Home 1.

Our Restaurant

At the Green Climbers Home restaurants “Kneebar” and “Layback Bar” you can dine in the chilled-out atmosphere or order food to take away to the crag (in multiuse-tupperware). We offer breakfast, lunch and dinner at reasonable prices. Bring your own bottle and please use the water refill at the Green Climbers Home.

LIVING AT THE CRAG

The resort ‘Green Climbers Home’ in Laos, Southeast Asia, is placed directly next to the climbing sectors. You find wonderful routes with tufas, sintering and face climbing. The gigantic roof with routes ranging from 6b+ to around 8c is unique. So far there is a total of 400 routes ranging from 4 to around 8c. Whether advanced climbers or beginners, you will have a lot of challenge and amusement! For the climbing beginners and advanced we offer climbing classes. For those who travel without climbing gear we can rent harnesses, climbing shoes, ropes, quickdraws, belay devices and helmets. Further on we sell tape, chalk and climbing ointment. We offer:

      • Brilliant climbing
      • Chilled-out atmosphere
      • Cozy bungalows
      • Delicious food
      • Climbing gear rental
      • Climbing courses
      • Yoga classes
      • Beach volleyball / Speedminton / Pétanque / Darts
