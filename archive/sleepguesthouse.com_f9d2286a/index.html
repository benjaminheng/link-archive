Welcome to Sleep Guesthouse

If what you’re looking for is a conveniently located hotel in Chiang Mai, look no further than Sleep Guesthouse. From here, guests can enjoy easy access to all that the lively city has to offer. With its convenient location, the hotel offers easy access to the city’s must-see destinations.

About Us

Sleep Guesthouse is a boutique guesthouse located in the heart of Chiang-Mai’s historical walled city. The laidback atmosphere makes the old city the perfect location for your stay in Chiang Mai.

Within the walled old city, visit some of the many beautifully serene temples or choose from a range of courses such as Thai Cookery, Yoga, Massage or Muay Thai.

Our Rooms

Sleep Guesthouse offers two kinds of rooms: Type one is the twin room with two super comfortable single beds and the second type is our room with a very comfortable Queen Size bed. On request we can also provide an extra bed for groups of three.

Frequently Asked Questions

My flight arrives early in the morning, what time can I check in?

You can check in from 14:00 onwards. If you do arrive earlier, please let us know so that we can check if it’s possible to check in earlier. Check out time is 12:00 (noon).

Do you provide an airport pick-up service?

Yes, we do offer an airport pick-up service from 8:00am till 8:00pm. You can select this option on the checkout page. If you arrive before or after the above mentioned times, please request an airport taxi at one of the taxi stands at the airport.

Is breakfast included as standard with all rooms?

Yes, breakfast for 2 is standard included with all rooms.
