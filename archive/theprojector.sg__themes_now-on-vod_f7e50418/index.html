Life is too short for bad flicks and chill.

With our streaming platform The Projector Plus – we’re bringing you everything you love about The Projector to your homes. Curated critically-acclaimed films, fresh cuts, film festivals – all in the Palm d’Or of your hands. Now you can make every movie count. Make any date night a special one, get some me-time in before bed, or just make it part of your W.F.H (Watch From Home) routine.

All movies are at $8 or $6 for a 48 hour viewing window, only available for Singapore-based viewers (unless specified otherwise). Keep a lookout for our bundle prices!

To start renting, you must create a Projector Plus account (it’s free!). This is not the same as your cinema membership – integration will take a while, once we can afford the peanuts to feed our tech monkeys.
