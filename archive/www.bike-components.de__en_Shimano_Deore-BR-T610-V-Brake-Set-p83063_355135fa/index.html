The Shimano Deore V-Brake BR-T610 Rim Brake Set - Reliability for trekking bikes

The BR-T610 V-brake set for front and rear wheel from the Shimano Deore series, unlike its predecessor, comes not only with 16 mm but also with 25 mm long M6 fixing bolts. It has 107 mm long brake arms made of anodised aluminium and cartridge brake shoe holders. The model not only looks good on your touring or trekking bike, it also offers reliable braking performance without too much noise. S70C pads and a 90-degree tension guide tube are included.
