I tried my brother-in-law's and thought it was such a genius idea, so I bought one. It's fine. It's pretty hollow and sort of cheap feeling, but not enough that I'm like afraid it'll break or something. And the rubber grips are a bit sticky, but it'll wear in as I play. The business is great, good shipping and prices. I think from a more personal opinion I just didn't think it changed things as much as I thought it would. It's more comfortable, but it's not like a game changer and now gaming is vastly better. It's fine. I will continue to use it because it is comfortable and convenient. If it broke I don't know that I would buy another. Also it does bother me that it is so large, I should have bought it with the case. So yeah. If you're looking for something cheap for someone that games I would get it, if you're on the fence about it I would buy it. It's worth it
