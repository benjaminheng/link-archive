Go Rock Climbing With Us In Paradise at Railay Beach, Krabi, Thailand!

Real Rocks Climbing customers get a FREE hotel pick up and drop off service from any hotel in Railay Beach, Krabi Town, Ao Nang & Klong Hang. Simply book your climbing adventure today and enter your hotel name at checkout! Each rock climbing tour includes:

  • small group experience for your safety and enjoyment,
  • climbing gear and equipment rental,
  • certified climbing guide
  • hotel pick up service from Ao Nang or Klong Hang (a surcharge may apply for pickups at hotels in other areas)
  • individual insurance for climbers
  • bottled water 

What to bring/wear:

  • small day backpack;
  • comfortable, loose clothing for climbing;
  • sun protection;
  • booking voucher or receipt;
  • camera;
  • waterproof bag and rain jacket (recommended for the rainy season);
  • closed toed shoes;
  • refillable water bottle;
  • and sandals or trekkers footwear for the ‘Full Day’ or ‘Family Fun’ trip to the caves.

1/2 Day Rock Climbing Tours at Railay Beach   ฿1200/adult, ฿1000/child for children ages 5-14.

Morning (9AM-1PM) OR Afternoon (1:30PM-5:30PM)

Get the perfect introduction to Krabi’s limestone cliffs on a half-day rock climbing tour at Railay Beach, Thailand.  Learn basic climbing techniques from our expert guides, then use a secure top rope to practice your climbing skills on the towering limestone cliffs overlooking the Andaman Sea, Railay and Phra Nang Beach. This tour is suitable for beginner and intermediate climbers, as well as children ages 5 and up. We provide small group tours with a maximum ratio of four climbers per climbing guide for your safety and enjoyment! Trips run year-round with daily morning and afternoon sessions.

If you’ve never climbed before this 4-hour introductory course is the ideal way to get yourself started. If you’ve already got some climbing experience, this trip will give you the chance to get out on the beautiful routes of Railay.  You will learn:

  • basic introduction to top rope sport rock climbing;
  • basic introduction to simple belay and rappel techniques;
  • basic outdoor climbing safety measures;
  • how to use climbing equipment;
  • basic climbing top rope movement and holding techniques.

Morning Half Day Rock Climbing Itinerary:

  • 7.30- 8.00 AM hotel pick up (optional)
  • 8.15 AM arrive at Real Rocks shop for check in and gear fitting
  • Transfer by taxi and long tail boat to Railay Beach (for customers on mainland only)
  • 9.15 AM-1.00 PM rock climbing on the beach
  • 1.00 PM end of climbing activity, (return by long tail boat to Ao Na Mao followed by drop off at hotel)

Afternoon Half Day Rock Climbing Itinerary:

  • 12.30- 1 PM hotel pick up (optional)
  • 1.15 PM arrive at Real Rocks shop for check in and gear fitting
  • Transfer by long tail boat to Railay Beach (for customers on mainland only)
  • 1.45 PM-5.30 PM rock climbing on the beach
  • 5.30 PM end of climbing activity, (return by long tail boat to Ao Na Mao followed by drop off at hotel)

(*Actual times may vary slightly from the schedule above.)

Full Day Rock Climbing And Caving Tours at Railay Beach in Krabi (9AM-5.30PM)   ฿2000/adult, ฿1700/child for children ages 7-14.

Scale the iconic limestone cliffs above Railay Beach on a full day rock climbing and caving tour in Krabi. Learn basic rock climbing skills from an expert guide, then get vertical in one of the world’s most scenic climbing destinations. This trip also includes caving, some light trekking, and an abseil (rappel) from the most beautiful viewpoint in Railay that towers 500-feet (150-meters) above the turquoise ocean! This tour is suitable for beginner and intermediate climbers, as well as children ages 7 and up. Trips run daily, year-round. Book Now!

You will learn:

  • basic introduction to top rope sport rock climbing;
  • basic introduction to simple belay and rappel techniques;
  • basic outdoor climbing safety measures;
  • how to use clumbing equipment;
  • basic climbing top rope movement and holding techniques;
  • caving and trekking in the national parks;
  • abseiling from the famous Railay viewpoint, enjoy a 10 meter abseil from the famous viewpoint 150 meters above sea level!

Full Day Rock Climbing Itinerary

  • 7.30- 8 AM hotel pick up (optional)
  • 8.15 AM meet at Real Rocks shop for check in and gear fitting
  • Transfer by taxi and long tail boat to Railay Beach (for customers on mainland only)
  • 9.15 AM-1.00 PM climbing on the beach
  • 1.00-1.45 PM break for lunch (lunch on your own)
  • 1.45-5.30 PM afternoon rock climbing session + caving & abseiling from the Railay viewpoint
  • 5.30 PM end of climbing activity, (return by long tail boat to Ao Na Mao followed by drop off at hotel)

(*Actual times may vary slightly from the schedule above.  Due to tides and other factors, we may choose to do the caving and abseiling in the morning session.)
