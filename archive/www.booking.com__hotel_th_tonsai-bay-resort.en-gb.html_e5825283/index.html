This property is 1 minute walk from the beach. Surrounded by greenery and cliffs, Tonsai Bay Resort is a 2-minute walk from Tonsai Beach and offers villas with private balconies. It features a diving centre, beachfront restaurant and bar.

Tonsai Bay Resort is reachable via boat only by a 10-minute boat ride from Aow-Nang Pier, which is a 30-minute drive from Krabi International Airport.

Each air-conditioned villa at the Tonsai Bay has a satellite TV, personal safe and minibar. Attached bathrooms offer hot showers. WiFi is available in restaurant and public area.

A rock climbing club lets guests explore the surrounding cliffs. Guests can enjoy a traditional Thai massage. A tour desk, food mart and laundry service are available.

Thai and international dishes are served at the Tonsai Bay’s restaurant.

Couples particularly like the location — they rated it 8.5 for a two-person trip.

  • How can I get to the closest beach from the property?

    Dear Sir/Madam, When it's low or mid-tide you can walk to West Railay. It takes 15- 20 minutes. However, the beach path is quite rocky. If it's high tide, you can take a longtail boat. Best regards, Tonsai Bay resort

  • Hello dear, How do we get to the resort from Ao Nang beach? How far is that and how much it costs? Thanks

    Dear Sir/Madam, You can take a public boat at Wangsai seafood restaurant or at Phranang inn curve to Tonsai bay. A ticket is 100 baht per person. The boat will leave with 8 passengers. Best regards, Tonsai Bay Resort

  • Hi, we plan to travel aboard to krabi and stay in tonsai beach area for a while. Wanna ask now how can we go from krabi town to tonsai beach? Thanks

    You can take a taxi or shuttle bus from Krabi town to Aonang beach. Then take a join boat to Tonsai Bay. The boat station is at Phranang inn curve. We also arrange the transfer pick up to the resort (Private boat).
